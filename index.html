Narad, by Daran Labs

Narad is a low-cost and easy to setup FM Transmission Mesh Network to accelerate the democratization of information sharing.

Internet information doesn't reach rural citizens for many reasons
Internet packages and Internet-enabled devices are often too expensive for rural users.
There is a lack of coverage in rural areas and companies are not incentivised enough to cover such areas.
The user interface often assumes literacy and is therefore alien to illiterate rural users.
Even if the user is literate, accurate, up-to-date information in the user's local language is often hard to find.

Our Solution

Narad

Narad is a WiFi mesh-based network of extremely cost-effective transmitters which amplify the FM signals to increase coverage.
Narad broadcasts are local and can be tuned into from any FM receiver device or common feature phones.
Narad devices are fully-configurable transmitters which can have optional add-ons such as screens and repeater nodes.

Narad

Narad costs only $13 per transmitter unit.
Narad broadcasts can be tuned into by common feature phones or other FM receivers easily.
Transmissions can be made in local languages, destroying the linguistic barrier.
Narad broadcasts can be accessed from a wide local area, due to mesh network.
